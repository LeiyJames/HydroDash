## Why HydroDash?

### The problem

- **Scattered orders** — WhatsApp, calls, and walk-ins make it hard to see what’s due and who delivered what.
- **Revenue at risk** — Cash from deliveries can go missing, and you have no clear record of what each rider should remit.
- **Manual tracking** — Bottles, loyalty, and promos live in spreadsheets or memory instead of one place.
- **Unclear status** — Customers keep asking “Where’s my order?” and you can’t give a simple answer.

### The solution

HydroDash puts **orders, customers, riders, and money** in one app. Customers order and track; you see revenue and expected COD from riders; riders update status from the field. Everything is recorded so you can run your business with confidence.

## Features That Improve Your Business

### For you (store owner / admin)

- **Dashboard that makes sense**  
  See total revenue, delivered orders, and pending orders at a glance. Know exactly how your store is performing.

- **Know who delivered what**  
  Filter orders by “Delivered by Rider” or “Delivered by Admin.” See which rider completed which delivery — no more guessing.

- **Expected COD from rider**  
  One number shows how much cash the rider should remit (today and this week) for COD orders they marked as delivered. Reconcile quickly and protect your profit.

- **Pricing and promos in your hands**  
  Set water prices, bottle deposit, and delivery charge once; they apply everywhere. Create promo codes with expiry and track how often they’re used.

- **Customer list that works**  
  View customers by store, see loyalty points and bottle balance, call or edit from the app. Deactivate when needed; reactivate when they return.

- **Activity log you can trust**  
  See who changed what and when — order status, pricing, vouchers, customer deactivation. Full trail for accountability.

- **Dark or light mode**  
  Use the app comfortably day or night, in the office or on the go.

### For your customers

- **Order in a few taps**  
  Choose water type (Alkaline, Distilled, Mineral, Purified), quantity, bottle returns, and delivery time. See the full price before paying.

- **Track every order**  
  Status updates from “Preparing” to “Out for Delivery” to “Delivered.” No more “Where’s my water?”

- **Loyalty that pays off**  
  Earn points on deliveries and redeem them for a free order. Build habit and reward regulars.

- **Promos that are easy to use**  
  Browse vouchers in the app and apply at checkout. Discounts and expiry are clear.

- **Receipts and history**  
  Every order has a receipt. Reorder from the last order with one tap. Disputes? Show the app.

- **Their choice: GCash or COD**  
  Pay online or cash on delivery. You decide what you accept; the app keeps the record.

### For your riders

- **One place for today’s work**  
  See pending orders, update status (Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered) from the field. No paper, no confusion.

- **My Deliveries**  
  List of orders they marked as Delivered, with day filter. Know what was completed and when.

- **Customer details when they need them**  
  Name, address, phone, and notes so riders can deliver and call if needed.

- **Clear accountability**  
  When they mark “Delivered,” it’s recorded with their identity and time. You and they both know who did what.

## FAQ (for website or in-app help)

**What is HydroDash?**  
HydroDash is a mobile app for water refilling businesses. Customers order and track delivery; store owners manage orders, pricing, and promos; riders update delivery status — all in one place.

**Do I need separate apps for customers and my team?**  
No. One app with three roles: Customer, Admin (you), and Rider. Everyone downloads the same app and signs in; the app shows the right dashboard for their role.

**How does HydroDash help prevent rider theft or lost cash?**  
Every order has a fixed total and payment method. You can filter “Delivered by Rider” and see “Expected COD from rider” (today and this week) — the amount they should remit. An activity log records who marked which order as delivered and when, so you can reconcile and follow up.

**Can customers pay online?**  
Yes. The app supports payment methods like GCash and Cash on Delivery (COD). You control what you accept; the app records it for each order.

**Can I run promos and loyalty?**  
Yes. Create promo codes with discount and expiry; customers see and apply them at checkout. Loyalty points are earned on deliveries and can be redeemed for a free order.
